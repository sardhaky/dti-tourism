Victoria Memorial Restoration Timeline

Overview

The Victoria Memorial is a large marble building in Kolkata, dedicated to Queen Victoria, and is one of the most famous landmarks in India.

Timeline of Key Events and Restoration Efforts

1921

The Victoria Memorial is inaugurated, serving as a symbol of the British Empire in India.

20th Century

The memorial undergoes several rounds of maintenance and minor restoration work to preserve its marble exterior and artifacts inside.

21st Century

The monument is actively conserved and restored by the Archaeological Survey of India, with periodic cleaning and structural preservation to maintain its condition.
