Sanchi Stupa Restoration Timeline

Overview

The Sanchi Stupa in Madhya Pradesh is one of the oldest stone structures in India, commissioned by Emperor Ashoka in the 3rd century BCE.

Timeline of Key Events and Restoration Efforts

3rd Century BCE

Original stupa commissioned by Emperor Ashoka.

1818

Rediscovered by British General Taylor.

1912

Restoration carried out under Sir John Marshall.

2023

Ongoing conservation to prevent erosion and structural damage.
