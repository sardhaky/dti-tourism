Great Living Chola Temples Restoration Timeline

Overview

These temples, located in Tamil Nadu, are masterpieces of Dravidian architecture and were built during the Chola dynasty (10th-12th centuries).

Timeline of Key Events and Restoration Efforts

1000-1200

Temples constructed under the Chola dynasty.

2004

UNESCO recognizes Chola Temples as World Heritage Sites.

2017

Restoration work undertaken to preserve sculptures and frescoes.

2023

Ongoing efforts to protect and maintain temple structures and artworks.
