Keesaragutta Temple

Restoration Timeline of Keesaragutta Temple

5th Century

Believed to be established by Lord Rama, dedicated to Lord Shiva.

1900s

Restoration of ancient idols and artifacts to preserve the heritage.

2000s

Accessibility improvements for pilgrims and tourism enhancements.

2022

Preservation of temple pillars and installation of visitor facilities.
