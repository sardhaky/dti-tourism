Konark Sun Temple Restoration Timeline

Overview

The Konark Sun Temple, located in Odisha, is a 13th-century temple dedicated to the Sun God, famous for its chariot shape and intricate carvings.

Timeline of Key Events and Restoration Efforts

1250

Temple constructed by King Narasimhadeva I of the Eastern Ganga Dynasty.

1903

British government initiated preservation efforts to prevent further deterioration.

1984

Recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

2023

Ongoing restoration to stabilize structure and restore sculptures.
