Hawa Mahal Restoration Timeline

Overview

Hawa Mahal, or the Palace of Winds, located in Jaipur, India, is a five-story palace known for its unique honeycomb structure.

Timeline of Key Events and Restoration Efforts

1799

Construction of Hawa Mahal by Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh.

1980s

Restoration efforts were undertaken to address damage from the harsh climate.

2022

Continued conservation work to preserve the palace's intricate architecture and structural integrity.
