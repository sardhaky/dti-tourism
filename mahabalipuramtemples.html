Mahabalipuram Temples Restoration Timeline

Overview

Mahabalipuram, located in Tamil Nadu, is home to a group of ancient rock-cut temples and sculptures built during the Pallava dynasty in the 7th and 8th centuries. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Timeline of Key Events and Restoration Efforts

7th Century

The Mahabalipuram Temples are constructed by the Pallava kings, including the Shore Temple and the Pancha Rathas.

17th Century

Parts of the temples suffer damage due to natural elements and neglect.

19th Century

British colonial authorities restore some of the temples and the famous Shore Temple.

21st Century

Further restoration work continues to preserve the temples' intricate carvings, sculptures, and structural integrity.
