Chilkur Balaji Temple

Restoration Timeline of Chilkur Balaji Temple

14th Century

Temple founded and dedicated to Lord Balaji near Osman Sagar Lake.

2000

Known as the "Visa Temple" due to devotees' belief in its wish-fulfillment powers.

2018

Infrastructure improvements and pilgrim facilities enhanced.

2023

Conservation of outer temple structure and murals completed.
