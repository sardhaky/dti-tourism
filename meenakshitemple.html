Meenakshi Temple Restoration Timeline

Overview

The Meenakshi Temple in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, is a historic temple complex dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Sundareswarar, with origins tracing back to ancient Tamil civilization.

Timeline of Key Events and Restoration Efforts

6th Century

Original construction by the early Tamil Pandya kings.

1623

Major expansion and renovation by King Tirumalai Nayak.

2020

Conservation efforts to protect intricate carvings and murals.

2023

Ongoing restoration to preserve temple architecture and artwork.
