Sustainable Tourism

Sustainable tourism minimizes negative environmental and cultural impacts, focusing on long-term benefits for communities and ecosystems.

  • Environmental Conservation: Reduce waste and promote biodiversity.
  • Support Local Communities: Encourage local economic growth by supporting businesses.
  • Cultural Preservation: Protect heritage sites and respect local customs.
