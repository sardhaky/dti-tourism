Gateway of India Restoration Timeline

Overview

The Gateway of India, located in Mumbai, was built to commemorate the visit of King George V and Queen Mary in 1911. It is an iconic symbol of India's history.

Timeline of Key Events and Restoration Efforts

1911

The Gateway of India is inaugurated during the visit of King George V and Queen Mary to India.

1947

The last British troops leave India through the Gateway, marking the end of British rule in India.

21st Century

The Gateway undergoes restoration to preserve its architecture, including cleaning and maintenance of the monument's structure and surroundings.
