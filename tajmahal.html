Taj Mahal Restoration Timeline

Overview

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, is an ivory-white marble mausoleum and a symbol of India's rich history and culture.

Timeline of Key Events and Restoration Efforts

1632

Construction of the Taj Mahal begins under the order of Emperor Shah Jahan.

1983

Declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

2020

Restoration of marble and minarets to prevent erosion and pollution damage.

2023

Ongoing preservation efforts to maintain structural integrity.
