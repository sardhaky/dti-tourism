Sri Ramalingeswara Swamy Temple

Restoration Timeline of Sri Ramalingeswara Swamy Temple

12th Century

Established during the Kakatiya dynasty, dedicated to Lord Shiva.

1990s

Initial restoration efforts focused on preserving temple architecture.

2005

Added visitor amenities and improved landscaping around the temple.

2022

Major conservation efforts to restore murals and ancient sculptures.
