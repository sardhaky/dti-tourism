Qutub Minar Restoration Timeline

Overview

The Qutub Minar in Delhi is a towering minaret and a UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its intricate carvings and inscriptions.

Timeline of Key Events and Restoration Efforts

1193

The Qutub Minar was built by Qutb-ud-Din Aibak.

1970s

Restoration works to stabilize the minaret and repair cracks in the structure.

2018

Ongoing conservation efforts to protect the monument from damage due to environmental factors.
