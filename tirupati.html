Tirupati Temple Restoration Timeline

Overview

The Tirupati Temple, dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, is located in Tirumala, Andhra Pradesh. It is one of the richest and most visited pilgrimage sites in the world.

Timeline of Key Events and Restoration Efforts

9th Century CE

The origin of the temple dates back to the 9th century under the rule of the Pallava dynasty.

11th Century

Reconstruction and enlargement of the temple under the Chola dynasty.

14th Century

The temple is further enhanced under the Vijayanagara Empire, with significant architectural developments.

20th Century

Major restoration and renovation efforts to preserve the sanctum sanctorum and the temple complex.

2009

The temple's modern infrastructure is enhanced with improved facilities for pilgrims.

Present Day

Ongoing efforts to maintain the temple's structure, enhance visitor experience, and preserve its spiritual significance.
