Charminar Restoration Timeline

Overview

The Charminar, located in Hyderabad, is a historic mosque and monument built in 1591, famous for its grand architecture and four towering minarets.

Timeline of Key Events and Restoration Efforts

1591

The Charminar is constructed by Sultan Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah to commemorate the founding of the city of Hyderabad.

19th Century

The Charminar undergoes minor restoration during the reign of the Nizams of Hyderabad.

21st Century

Restoration work includes structural repairs, cleaning, and preservation of the monument's intricate carvings and archways.
