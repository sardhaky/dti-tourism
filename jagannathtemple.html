Jagannath Temple

Restoration Timeline of Jagannath Temple

2009

Construction of Jagannath Temple inspired by the Puri Jagannath Temple.

2011

Inauguration of the temple with Lord Jagannath, Balabhadra, and Subhadra idols.

2015

Annual Rath Yatra festival celebrated for the first time.

2021

Red sandstone preservation to enhance and maintain temple architecture.
